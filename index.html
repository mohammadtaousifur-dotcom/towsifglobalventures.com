About Us

Towsif Global Ventures LLC is a U.S.-registered limited liability company based in Wyoming. The company focuses on e-commerce retail, consumer product sourcing, and marketplace-based distribution.

Our approach is built around responsible sourcing, supplier communication, accurate product documentation, and compliance-focused retail operations.

Supplier Partnerships

Towsif Global Ventures LLC is open to working with qualified suppliers, wholesalers, distributors, and manufacturers of consumer products.

We value clear communication, accurate invoices, product authenticity, compliant documentation, and long-term supplier relationships.

What We Look For

  • Proper business invoices
  • Clear product identifiers such as UPC, model, size, and variation
  • Transparent pricing and minimum order quantities
  • Product compliance information where required
  • Reliable fulfillment and shipping communication
